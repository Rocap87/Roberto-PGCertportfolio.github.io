What is a Secure Programming Language? - Unit 3

For this activity we had to read Chapter 2,6,7,8 of the course text (Pillai, 2017) and Cifuentes & Bierman (2019) and then answer the questions below, adding them as evidence to your e-portfolio.

  • What factors determine whether a programming language is secure or not?
  • Could Python be classed as a secure language? Justify your answer.
  • Python would be a better language to create operating systems than C. Discuss.

End of Module Assignment

This is a final module essay assessment to examine the awareness of current affairs, challenges, technological developments, trends and issues in relation to recent development in computer science. The essay fulfils the learning outcome where I can critically appraise the emerging trends in the field of computer science and the professional and ethical requirements for dealing with such contemporary computer-based technologies.

The title of my essay is: THE USE OF BIG DATA IN FORMULA 1 AND BY THE MERCEDES AMG PETRONAS F1 TEAM

The report analyses the impact of big data in Formula 1 (F1). In the modern era of F1, big data is assuming an important role in attracting more fans, helping with developing cars with better aerodynamic solutions, and influencing the strategies adopted by teams during race weekends to win grand prix and the world title championship. After discussing the role of big data more generally, this report outlines how big data is used both by F1 and the Mercedes AMG Petronas F1 Team (Mercedes).
